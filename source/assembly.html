Assembly¶

The field assembly includes nine entries that aim at describing the overall configuration of the wind turbine:

    turbine_class: I
    turbulence_class: B
    drivetrain: direct_drive
    rotor_orientation: Upwind
    number_of_blades: 3
    hub_height: 150.
    rotor_diameter: 242.23775645
    rated_power: 15.e+6
    lifetime: 25.0
turbine_classString

IEA wind class. The entry should be I, II, III, or IV.

turbulence_classString

IEA turbulence class. The entry should be A, B, or C.

drivetrainString

Drivetrain configuration. This is intended to inform a automated interpreter of the yaml about the data specified in the field drivetrain

rotor_orientationString

Switch between upwind and downwind rotor configurations.

number_of_bladesInteger

Number of rotor blades.

hub_heightFloat, m

Height of the hub center from the ground or from the mean sea level

rotor_diameterFloat, m

Rotor diameter, defined as the sum of hub diameter and two times the blade length along its z axis, see Blade

rated_powerFloat, W

Electrical rated power of the wind turbine

lifetimeFloat, yr

Design lifetime of the turbine
